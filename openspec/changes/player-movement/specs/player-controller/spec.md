# Player Controller

## Requirements

- The player moves left and right with arrow keys (or A/D)
- The player jumps with Space (or Z) when grounded; double-jump is not allowed
- Horizontal movement decelerates to zero when no key is held (friction)
- Gravity pulls the player down continuously; terminal velocity is not required for v1
- Pressing X cycles through 6 character appearances; the visual changes immediately
- The player sprite flips horizontally to face the direction of movement
- The player animates (cycles frames) while moving and holds a single frame while idle
- The player does not pass through solid geometry
